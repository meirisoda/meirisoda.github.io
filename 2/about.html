soda (meirisoda, mei).
I am an interdisciplinary artist whose focus is to find closure and healing through art and music.


I typically create illustrations. The meaning of my art is up to your interpretation - my art is a way for me to put my emotions onto paper.

I use Clip Studio Paint, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and Ibispaint for illustrations/graphics. I hope that I will be able to create art through Blender as well as 3js and p5js.
