Look! The gingko leaf looks more full of life now.

Maybe its realization that it has reason and a place helped it grow.
The process of realization can be very slow.


It's like watering a plant - it won't grow immediately.
A plant needs proper care and nutrition in order to grow. An environment with patience and understanding and warmth.




I like to think that I water myself.
It might not be every day; I may skip or forget some days, weeks or even months.


But the gingko leaf is resilient.
 It continues.
