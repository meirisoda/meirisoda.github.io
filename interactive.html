This is my first ever fully self-coded website that I've made.
Prior to this, I've only ever used site hosting clients such as
blogspot and tumbl. I found that I can never really get what I
wanted my websites to look like, and even though I could
edit the html/css to my liking, those sites are limited by the
function of the hosting site - so for tumblr, everything is post
based, and I would have to post and move things around with
the post-based system.

With a fully self-coded website, I have free reign over everything
that I want my website to host. This github pages site is the site
that I learned html/css/js with and I am very proud of it. I think
that it looks very clean and minimal, but doesn't represent me
as well as this one (https://meirisoda.github.io) does.
